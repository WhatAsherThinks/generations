Generations

See the progress of time through Generation. This platform tracks the past, present, and future through images. We see how culture transforms as different age groups are placed side by side. Can you see the similarities of the elderly and kin? How do today's children compare to those of just a decade ago? How about five decades ago? Here we document history by taking note of the present. Everyone is part of a Generation, this one included.
